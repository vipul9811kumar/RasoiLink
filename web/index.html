The Problem

A broken system
hurting everyone.

The Indian restaurant industry in the US runs on word-of-mouth, WhatsApp groups, and handshake deals — leaving workers exploited and owners stuck in chaos.

👨‍🍳

For Workers

  • 💸
    Wage theft & delayed pay Paid late, underpaid, or not at all — with no paper trail or recourse.
  • 🌐
    Language barriers Job postings are in English only. Workers who speak Hindi, Telugu, or Punjabi can't navigate existing platforms.
  • 🏚️
    No accommodation transparency Housing promises made verbally, often broken on arrival.
  • 😶
    No ratings or reputation Workers can't vet employers before taking risky cross-state moves.
🏪

For Owners

  • 👻
    No-show workers Staff vanish without notice, leaving kitchens understaffed on peak nights.
  • 🔍
    Zero vetting tools Hiring through referrals and gut feel — no skill verification, no work history.
  • 📱
    WhatsApp chaos Hiring happens across hundreds of fragmented group chats with no organization.
  • 📋
    No operational structure No contracts, no accountability framework — legally and operationally exposed.

Trust & Transparency

The trust layer
this industry never had.

RasoiLink isn't a scheduling app. It's trust infrastructure — built to protect both sides of every working relationship.

💳

Pay Verification

Workers confirm receipt of each paycheck. Owners build a verified pay track record. Disputes are flagged and mediated — no more wage theft in the shadows.

📋

Digital Work Agreements

Simple, plain-language agreements in your language. Both parties sign digitally. No lawyers needed — but everyone's protected.

Two-Way Ratings

Workers rate employers. Employers rate workers. A transparent reputation system that rewards reliability and surfaces bad actors.

Departure Notice System
